Try butterbrod, a russian favorite

Butterbrod is a russian based dish that is bread with butter, cheese, sausage, sometimes tomato, and maybe some spices.

It's a great dish and very good as breakfast, lunch, and dinner, so go ahead, enjoy it any time of day!
